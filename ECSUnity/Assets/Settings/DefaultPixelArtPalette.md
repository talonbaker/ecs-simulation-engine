# DefaultPixelArtPalette

16-color 1×16 RGBA PNG. Assign to `PixelArtRendererFeature.Settings.paletteTexture`.

| Index | Hex     | Role                         |
|------:|---------|------------------------------|
|     0 | #1a1a2e | Shadow / deep background     |
|     1 | #16213e | Dark navy                    |
|     2 | #0f3460 | Medium navy                  |
|     3 | #2d4a7a | Steel blue                   |
|     4 | #4a7c9e | Sky blue                     |
|     5 | #7eb9d6 | Light blue — CRT highlight   |
|     6 | #c8d4b4 | Beige-white                  |
|     7 | #e8d5a3 | Warm beige — CRT off-white   |
|     8 | #c4a882 | Tan / sand                   |
|     9 | #8b6347 | Brown                        |
|    10 | #4a3728 | Dark brown                   |
|    11 | #ff6b6b | Chibi accent — red           |
|    12 | #ffd93d | Chibi accent — yellow        |
|    13 | #6bcb77 | Chibi accent — green         |
|    14 | #ff9a3c | Chibi accent — orange        |
|    15 | #c77dff | Chibi accent — purple        |

Palette is biased toward CRT-era beige/blue (indices 0–10) with saturated
chibi accents (11–15), per aesthetic-bible §"Era-appropriate palette."

Iteration planned post-PT-NNN playtest session.
