cast-bible.md

Eight beats per archetype:

* Name + role — self-explanatory.
* The one thing. The defining trait in one sentence. ("Donna treats every conversation as a chance to vent.")
* Three elevated drives. From the eventual social-drive list (belonging, status, affection, irritation, attraction, trust, loneliness, etc.) — which three are chronically elevated for them? This is what makes them them in ECS terms.
* Personality numbers. Big Five works fine: openness, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, neuroticism — each on −2 to +2. This compiles directly into a personality component.
* Schedule shape. When are they at their desk? When do they wander? Who do they pass? Where do they take lunch? This drives the schedule subsystem.
* Five sample utterances. Not one catchphrase — five. Greeting, complaint, agreement, frustration, joy. One catchphrase is a tic; five samples is a voice that dialogue templates can expand from without going repetitive within a play session.
* Starting relationships. One line per other named NPC. "Frank: rival, they had it out in 2019." "Linda: secret crush, never spoken about."
* Visible silhouette. Tall? Short? Bald? Glasses? Distinctive shirt color? Not a sprite spec — the silhouette a player should learn within an hour.
